<b>0.1a</b> - 
  * First public release
  * Support AC3/DTS files
  * Formats supported:
      AC3: 8 bit (equivalent to 16bit little endian, usual format)
           16 bit big endian
      DTS: 8 bit (equivalent to 16bit little endian, usual format)
           14 bit big/little endian, padded/unpadded,
           16 bit big endian
  * Support for AC3/AudioCD and DTS/AudioCD format
    (SPDIF padded format, IEC 61937)
